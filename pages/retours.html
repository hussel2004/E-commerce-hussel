Return Policy

Your satisfaction is important to us. This page explains the procedure and conditions for returning a product purchased on DigitalStore.

Withdrawal Period

You have a period of 14 calendar days from the date of receipt to notify us of your wish to return a product (right of withdrawal). For returns due to defects or non-compliance, please contact us as soon as possible — specific deadlines may apply (e.g., 7 days) depending on the situation.

Conditions for Return Acceptance

  • The product must be unused or in the same condition as received (unless there is a visible defect).
  • Original packaging, manuals, cables, accessories, and tags must be included.
  • Provide proof of purchase (order number, invoice, or receipt).

Non-Returnable Products / Exclusions

Returns may be refused in the following cases:

  • Products whose security seal has been broken (e.g., activated software/digital products).
  • Customized or specially configured products.
  • Items damaged due to misuse or customer negligence.
  • Opened accessories and consumables (e.g., certain films or disposable accessories).

Accepted Reasons for Return/Replacement

We accept returns for the following reasons:

  • Defective product upon receipt.
  • Product not matching the website description.
  • Missing parts in the box.

Return Procedure (Steps)

  1. Contact Customer Service: Send an email to retours@digitalstore.example or call +237 6X XX XX XX with your order number and reason for return.
  2. We will send you a Return Authorization Number (RMA) and shipping instructions.
  3. Carefully pack the product in its original packaging with all accessories and proof of purchase.
  4. Ship the package to the address we provide or use the return label (if available).
  5. Once received, we will inspect the product. If the return is approved, we will issue a refund or exchange as per your choice.

Note: Keep your proof of shipment until your refund is processed.

Return Fees

- If the return is due to a defect, preparation error, or non-compliance on our part: return costs are covered by us (prepaid label or reimbursement of shipping fees upon proof).
- For a simple change of mind: shipping costs are the customer’s responsibility unless otherwise specified by a promotion or local policy.

Refund Time and Method

Once the product is received and inspected, refunds are processed within 3 to 10 business days, depending on the original payment method. Refunds will preferably be made using the same payment method used at purchase.

Warranty & After-Sales Service (Customer Support)

Products sold are covered by the manufacturer’s warranty, where applicable. If a defect appears after the withdrawal period, please contact our support team to open a warranty claim.

Quick FAQ

What should I do if I received a damaged product?
Contact us immediately (email + photos of the package/product). We’ll arrange a return and replacement or refund.
Can I exchange one model for another?
Yes, subject to availability. Shipping fees may apply if the exchange is not due to our error.
How long do I have to report a defect?
Report any defect as soon as possible — ideally within 7 days of receipt to facilitate processing.
